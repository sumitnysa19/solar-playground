# Overview
Approximate scale reading (kgf) of a 1 kg mass on surfaces/cloud-tops of Solar System bodies. Values derive from surface gravity relative to Earth (g_body/9.81).

# 1 kg Earth Mass → Scale Reading
- Mercury: ~0.38 kgf
- Venus: ~0.90 kgf
- Earth: 1.00 kgf
- Moon: ~0.17 kgf
- Mars: ~0.38 kgf
- Jupiter (1 bar level): ~2.53 kgf
- Saturn (1 bar level): ~1.06 kgf
- Uranus (cloud tops): ~0.89 kgf
- Neptune (cloud tops): ~1.14 kgf
- Pluto: ~0.06 kgf

Note: Gas/ice giants have no solid surface; values reference standard pressure levels (e.g., ~1 bar) where “weight” is conceptual.
